InnovBots is a startup that aims to create innovative solutions for businesses and societies. We believe that innovation can make a great change in how businesses and societies work.

Marketplaces - whether local, regional, national or global - are becoming highly competitive. Due to the lack of innovation, organizations are experimenting a falling productivity and efficiency. Unfortunately, Employees are not empowered to innovate in many organizations.


Our main solution, called innovbot is an idea management platform using a chatbot for collecting insights and ideas from employees for the continuous improvement of an existing process or a product.

Innovbot is based on creative tools to empower the user to innovate. All interactions with users are anonymous, so that can feel free to share insights and ideas for the improvement of a product or a process within their organization. Innovbot is designed to collect ideas from users who have already an idea, but also it can guide employees who don't have an initial idea in mind to get inspired and create new ones. All ideas are then inserted into a database so that those ideas can be taken to the next steps.
